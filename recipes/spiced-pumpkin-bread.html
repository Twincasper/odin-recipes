Spiced pumpkin bread

Description

Apparently pumpkin bread is a thing and Buddy can eat it, so today we're writing about a pumpkin loaf that is spiced with cinnamon, nutmeg, and ground cloves.

Ingredients

  1. 1 ½ cups white sugar
  2. 1 cup light brown sugar
  3. 1 cup butter, melted
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  6. 3 cups pumpkin puree
  7. 4 cups all-purpose flour
  8. 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  9. 1 tablespoon baking powder
  10. 1 tablespoon baking soda
  11. 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  12. ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  13. ½ teaspoon salt
